About Us

How We Met:
We started at GE Digital in San Ramon in 2013. Ashley quickly became friends with Pawel and Cameron and Nik brought his own friends, Andy and Minh.
After some subtle hinting on Ashley's part, directed at Nik... Nothing happened. Finally, on February 13th, Nik got the memo and we had our first Valentine's together.
We'd like to share (and formalize) that anniversary with all of you.





Our Engagement:

Several happy and scenic steps later, Nik found a much better vista and decided to ‘Write his own history’. Ben obliged and took these gorgeous photos.


Our Future:
